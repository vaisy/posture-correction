ABC™ Seat Wedges

We are proud to be able to offer the new and improved one and two inch seat wedges courtesy of the European ABC™ Association. These seat wedges help to keep your pelvis in a neutral position to ensure you unwind quicker and get well sooner. They eliminate adverse torque and force on your spine by helping keep you upright.

ABC™ Heel Chips

These chips are designed to be worn in the heel of your shoes. Believe it or not a slight heel is actually good for you. Unlike regular heel shoes however, that cause your calcaneus (heel bone) to tilt backwards, these chips keep your calcaneus in a more optimum position. Much like our seat wedges the heels chips help promote a more neutral spine position.

ABC™ Heel Chips

These are variation on the heel chips but work by the same principle of keep your spine neutral. They are more customisable therefore can be tailored to suit you and your body.

ABC™ Arch Support Dot

These dots have been designed and engineered to be worn in the BREAKDOWN side foot. They help keep your foot on that side from collapsing and therefore maintain a neutral spine. Unlike orthotics however they do not artificially raise your arch but instead provide stimulus so you raise the arch yourself, strengthening muscles and tendons as you do so. Orthotics is actually unhealthy and simply helps you compensate more for mechanical pathologies. In doing so you actually get worse over time, mechanically.

ABC™ Pillow

Through extensive research and development Dr Jutkowitz came up with what all ABC Practitioner's would consider to be the perfect pillow. It comes in layers of special foam that are just the right density to provide enough support and comfort for your head as you sleep. As the layers are different thickness the pillow can be tailored to suit YOU and YOUR spine. Marketing campaigns that promote pillows that keep you and your head level are incorrect in their understanding. Your spine configuration is unique to you, meaning a one size fits all pillow is not the right solution. As you get treatment your requirements for the height of the pillow will change over time. If you ever wake up with headaches or in the middle of the night and can't get back to sleep again your pillow height is wrong. When you set up the ABC Pillow correctly and couple it with the correct mattress you will fall asleep faster and enjoy better sleep than you have ever had. Ask your practitioner for further information.

ABC™ Mattress

These mattresses have been designed to be very firm in order to provide the necessary support your body and spine need. When your mattress is soft it allows too much movement in the vertebrae of your spine and promotes adverse curvatures. This is why it is often difficult or even painful at times to get out of bed after a long night. Think of it this way; is it easier to walk and move on hard floors such as concrete or on loose sand such as at the beach? Similarly to how taxing it can be to walk across sand as your feet slip and sink your body can get taxed in soft mattresses as you sleep, roll and change position. Ask your practitioner for further information.
