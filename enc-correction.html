What is ECC?


video courtesy of KenHub.

ECC or Endo-Nasal Cranial Correction is a well known technique within bone and body manipulation that involves inserting a small balloon within the nasal cavities to manipulate the position of the sphenoid bone. This is a butterfly shaped bone that spans the width of the skull and lies behind the eyes and nose. When out of optimum position, senses such as vision, smell and even taste can be affected.


How we differ from regular chiropractic and other health practitioner's in using this technique is that we target only the cavities or conchas that NEED doing. Making it more precise and effective. Other's use a more scatter gun approach and do all six instead. This could be uncomfortable and even mechanically adverse. Dizziness, nausea, blurry vision are some of the adverse symptoms.
