Select Your Plan

As everybody's body is different some of you will need more or less treatment than others. We have several pricing structures in place based upon the treatment plan you need, therefore ensuring you get the care you need at a price you can afford.

Please note however that any prospective patient has to undergo a mandatory screening and assessment before any treatment can be delivered. This involves thorough gathering of history through survey and interview, followed by physical examination to determine aspects of your current health including peak flow (lung capacity), range of motion and flexibility. We may also ask about your mental health to determine how you will cope with unwinding. The initial exam and assessment is also used to determine your breakdown side and includes a treatment if you wish on that day. It could take up to 90 mins therefore will cost £69. To save time please ensure you complete and return all the documents that would have been emailed to you before your assessment.

In terms of treatment plans themselves it is generally recommended to be treated at least twice a week to get the most effective rate of progression in your health. Your individual needs can be discussed further with you during consultation.
