    Treatment Questions

  • Is ABC™ the same as chiropractic?

    No, they are not quite the same. Chiropractic generally speaking only tackles your body's compensations, whereas ABC gets to the route of the problem(s) so your body no longer needs it compensations.

  • How much does it cost?

    We have different pricing plans depending on the level of treatment you need and how long for. See 'Treatment Plans' page for more details.

  • How often do I need treatment?

    This depends on your body. Generally speaking it is recommended to have treatment twice a week for the most optimum recovery.

  • How long must I get treated for?

    Again this depends on your body. On average, a typical adult would have unwound and recovered enough after 2-3 years assuming twice weekly appointments kept. This may be more for some and less for others.

  • Do you treat the same things over again?

    No. We only treat what needs to be done on that day. On another day we may need to go back to some areas we have already done but each appointment will need different areas treated. Some sessions could be longer than others.

  • Is ABC™ safe for younger people i.e. children?

    Yes, absolutely. ABC™ is safe and recommended for all ages of people and quite safe. As the manoeuvres are slow in nature, the protocol is actually safer than most regular chiropractic techniques.

  • Does it hurt?

    Some manoeuvres, in particular the meningeal releases, can be a little uncomfortable at times but as long as you remain loose and relaxed it is not painful.

  • Do you offer parking on site?

    There is a limited amount of parking available on site but there is more public parking available on the side streets nearby.

  • Can I cancel/reschedule my appointment if I can't make it?

    Of course, as long as we have 24 hours notice we can reschedule your appointment to a more convenient time. Please note however MISSED appointments will not be rescheduled.

  • Do I have to follow any special exercise program or advice?

    No. You can carry on about your normal daily routine. We do recommend however that you follow the SSS advice, i.e. how to sit, stand, and sleep optimally for the most effective recovery.

  • Must I come on different days if I get treated more than once a week?

    Ideally we'd recommend a few days between treatments to maximize unwinding between treatments, however if you can only make it on one day out of the week and you need 2 or more treatments, you can space them by an hour or more.

  • Do I HAVE to buy your posture products?

    No, of course not. However, they are recommended for optimum unwinding and recovery. The mattress and pillow in particular are highly recommended, as well as the seat wedge if you stay seated for long periods of time.
